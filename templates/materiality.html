Materiality Calculation Help
How Materiality is Calculated:

Materiality is calculated based on a percentage of the selected benchmark. The formula is:

Materiality = Benchmark Value Ã— Materiality Percentage

Benchmark Types:
  • Total Column: Uses the sum of all values in the selected column
  • Specific Accounts: Uses the sum of values from accounts matching the selected mapping
Performance Materiality:

Performance materiality is typically set at 50-75% of overall materiality to provide a buffer for undetected misstatements.

Account Scoping:

Accounts are considered in scope if they meet any of these criteria:

  • Balance exceeds performance materiality
  • High risk of material misstatement
  • Significant risk factors present
